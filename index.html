Prioritization roadmap

Four phases to full automation

Phase 1

Point-of-care discovery

Build the FHIR-native gateway and launch real-time coverage lookup (CRD) and automated documentation checklists (DTR) inside provider EHRs.

Phase 2

Automated submission & intake

Roll out standardized PA submission APIs to eliminate legacy web portals and manual fax ingestion.

Phase 3

Rules-based auto-decisioning

Deploy the deterministic policy engine to instantly auto-approve high-volume, highly compliant codes.

Phase 4

AI clinical review & care coordination

Apply NLP to unstructured chart notes for exception routing, linked into longitudinal care management.
